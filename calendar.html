Schedule & closures

Calendar & hours

Day-to-day dates, classroom events, and reminders are shared with enrolled families through the ProCare app and our monthly newsletter. Here are the essentials.

🕖

Hours of operation

Open Monday–Friday, 7:30 am to 5:30 pm. We offer three part-time schedule options: Monday–Friday, Monday/Wednesday/Friday, or Tuesday/Thursday.

📱

How dates are shared

Enrolled families receive the current calendar, event reminders, and classroom updates through the ProCare app and a monthly newsletter. Family–teacher conferences are held twice a year.

Inclement weather

Severe-weather closures

The center closes when Seattle Public Schools close due to severe weather. During delayed openings, the center operates on its normal schedule.

On non-school days, the Executive Director decides whether the center will close, with notification posted to our website and voicemail.

  • SPS closed for weather → WCCC is closed
  • SPS delayed start → WCCC operates normally
  • Non-school days → Director decides; check website & voicemail
  • Questions on a closure? Call 206.632.3200
