Home / Kitchen

Nourishing every day

Made on-site, shared together

All snacks and meals served at WCCC are prepared on-site by our cook, who is responsible for our monthly menus.

Mealtimes

Food is part of the curriculum

Children eat together in their classrooms and take part in meal preparation and cleanup. Sharing food builds community, independence, and confidence — and it's a joyful part of every day.

  • Snacks and meals prepared fresh, on-site, each day
  • Monthly menus planned by our cook
  • Children help with preparation and cleanup

Allergy care

Safe meals for every child

For children with food allergies, we work with parents to find food substitutes and prepare meals they're able to eat. When possible, we make alternatives in-house that are similar to the regular menu items.

  • Substitutes prepared in-house when possible
  • Labeled plates identify each child's name and the allergens they avoid
  • Close partnership with families on every child's needs
