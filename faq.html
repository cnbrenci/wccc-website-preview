Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Answers for current and prospective families. Don't see what you need? Get in touch — we're happy to help.

For current families

What should my child bring each day?

Please bring extra clothes for your child in case of accidents, and appropriate rain gear (boots, rain pants, coat, etc.) — we play outside, even in the rain! A labeled water bottle is essential, especially during summer. Please label all of your child's belongings.

When should I keep my child home?

Children should not attend if they are experiencing any of the following:

  • Vomiting two or more times in 24 hours
  • A body rash with fever
  • Diarrhea (three or more watery stools in 24 hours)
  • An eye infection with discharge
  • A sore throat with fever
  • Unusual fatigue or paleness
  • A fever of 100°F or higher
What if my child becomes sick during the day?

Please arrange backup childcare in case your child needs to be picked up, and contact the center at 206-632-3200 with any questions.

What is the severe-weather closure policy?

The center closes when Seattle Public Schools close due to severe weather. During delayed openings, the center operates normally. On non-school days, the Executive Director decides whether to close, with notification posted to our website and voicemail. See the Calendar page for details.

For prospective families

What ages do you serve?

We welcome children beginning at 8 weeks old, through 5 years of age.

What schedule options are available?

Our center is open 7:30 am–5:30 pm, with three part-time options: Monday–Friday, Monday/Wednesday/Friday, or Tuesday/Thursday.

How do I schedule a tour?

Email directorwccc@gmail.com to request a group tour. Tours are available for families on the waitlist or with a child between 2 and 5 years old.

How does the waitlist work?

Submit a waitlist application via Kiddosoftware. A non-refundable application fee applies (waived for families who qualify for subsidy). Priority is based on your original submission date.

Do I need to call to stay on the waitlist?

No periodic check-in calls are needed. Simply keep your information current through your parent profile.

Still have questions?

We're glad to talk through anything about enrollment, schedules, or daily life at the center.
