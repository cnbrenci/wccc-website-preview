Why WCCC

A unique, family-oriented community

Wallingford Childcare Center is a unique family-oriented community. We are proud to have low ratios, offer great benefits, and work in a collaborative and supportive community of educators and families.

We believe caregivers deserve respect, empathy, and kindness — and that the best learning happens when teachers feel supported, too.
