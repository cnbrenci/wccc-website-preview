Step one

Schedule a tour

We offer group tours of the center for prospective families. To schedule a tour, please email us if you are on the waitlist or have a child between 2 and 5 years old.

Email to book a tour

Step two

Join the waitlist

Families with children in classes that have a waitlist will be contacted as spots become available. To join, complete a waitlist application.

  • $79.50 non-refundable application fee — waived for families who qualify for subsidy.
  • Priority is based on your original application submission date.
  • Keep your details current through your parent profile — no check-in calls needed.
